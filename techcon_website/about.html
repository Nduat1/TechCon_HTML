    • History of TechCon

    TechCon started in 2005 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts eager to explore the latest innovations in software and hardware development. Over the years, it has evolved into a global event, bringing together thousands of developers, entrepreneurs, and thought leaders to discuss cutting-edge technology.

    The early years of TechCon focused primarily on open-source software and community-driven development. However, as technology advanced, the conference expanded its scope to include AI, machine learning, blockchain, and sustainable tech innovations. Today, TechCon is known for being a hub of inspiration and collaboration in the tech industry.

    Our Mission

    TechCon is dedicated to fostering innovation, collaboration, and knowledge-sharing in the global tech community. Our mission is to provide a platform for thought leaders and innovators to explore new ideas, share their expertise, and inspire the next generation of technological breakthroughs.

    We aim to create an inclusive environment where professionals from all walks of life can engage in meaningful dialogue, learn from industry experts, and contribute to solving real-world problems through technology.

    At TechCon, we believe that technology should be a force for good, driving positive change in society and helping to build a more sustainable future. We encourage attendees to challenge the status quo and think critically about how we can leverage technology for the greater good.

    Past Speakers

    Elon Musk

    CEO of SpaceX and Tesla, Elon Musk is a visionary entrepreneur who has transformed multiple industries through technological innovation. His keynote speech at TechCon 2020 focused on sustainable energy and space exploration, offering insights into the future of renewable energy and interplanetary travel.

    Susan Wojcicki

    As the CEO of YouTube, Susan Wojcicki has been a leader in digital media and online content creation. At TechCon 2018, she discussed the future of content platforms, the role of AI in media, and the importance of supporting creators in the digital age.
